About Us

Filosophy

ProSeg makes available to its clients a personalized Integrated Management System (IMS) for the service it provides to each of them.

The IMS adjusts to the needs and requirements of each client, on the basic principles of:

  • Analysis of the occupational risk assessment.
  • Logistics needs analysis.
  • Proposal of various product alternatives.
  • Proposal for logistics-type solutions.
  • Post-sale assistance.
  • Technical support to support customer training plans.
  • Organization of sectorized information events.
